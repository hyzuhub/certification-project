Become Familiar with Your Fighting Arena




You'll be an expert on chess rules in less than an hour. Even if you don't know the first thing about chess. You'll be ready to wade into battle in no time.

Developing good, sound strategy may take a while, but the rules are a snap. The game of chess is played between two opponents on a square board called a chess board. The object of the game is to capture the opposing King.

The player with the white pieces starts the game. Play then alternates with each player taking turns to move.
