The Laws on Move Completion




Some of the following sections apply to playing over the board in the offline world. You'll know which ones as you read them. They won't effect your online games. A move is completed in the case of:

  1. a move to a vacant square, when your hand has released the piece

  2. a capture, when the captured piece has been removed and your hand has released your piece

  3. castling, when your hand has released the Rook

  4. a promotion of a pawn, when the pawn has been removed from the board and your hand has released the new Queen
